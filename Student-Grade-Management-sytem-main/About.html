OVERVIEW OF THE SCHOOL

Kapkolei Girls Senior School is a premier public boarding institution located in the Aldai Constituency of Nandi County, Kenya. As a designated Senior School under the Kenyan education system, it offers specialized education for students in Grades 10, 11, and 12.

Managed by a dedicated Board of Management (BOM), the school is committed to academic excellence and moral uprightness, following the vision instilled by its founders that education is the ultimate enabler of progress.
